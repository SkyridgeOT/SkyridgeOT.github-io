Analogue House

Analogue House is a service in development. Based in Richmond, the purpose of Analogue House is to provide mental health and wellbeing support to sex workers experiencing vocational burnout.

Still in development, we hope to offer various modalities of care, including counseling under mental health care plans, respite, and peer support. Funds are currently being sought to make repairs and create a welcoming environment.

Analogue House is a passion project with no funding or financial capital. Please help contribute to this vision by purchasing a 2025 Analogue House Calendar.
